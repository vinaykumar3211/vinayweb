Logo usage

Use the primary mark on dark or neutral backgrounds with required clear space. Do not skew, recolor, or add effects.

Do

  • Keep clear space equal to logo cap height.
  • Use provided assets; maintain aspect ratio.
  • Ensure contrast passes WCAG against background.

Don’t

  • Recolor the mark or add shadows/glows.
  • Stretch, skew, or rotate the logo.
  • Place on busy imagery without a solid backing.
